United Nation Security Council (UNSC)

The United Nation Security Council was founded alongside with the United Nations itself back in 1945, it has primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security. The Security Council works to eliminate threats to peace or acts of aggression. All Member States are obligated to comply with Council decisions under the UN Charter.
