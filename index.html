Director's Message

Dear SCMUN Attendees,

To our guests from elsewhere, welcome to Nansha College Preparatory Academy! We are very honored to host you at this event, which we have proudly sponsored since our school's founding in 2012. As NCPA students will tell you, the mission of this school is to ensure that our students achieve academic and social excellence. I can think of no co-curricular activity that better aligns with our mission than Model UN!

We aim for NCPA students to become effective negotiators, collaborative learners, resourceful planners and principled achievers. In the next two days at our school, you all will consider the great, global issues of the day; you will work together to craft solutions; you will negotiate with those who are affected; and you will achieve consensus on your resolutions. I wish you a productive and meaningful experience here!

Michael Cyrus, Ed.D.

Director

Nansha College Preparatory Academy
