Rules and Procedures

This is the New Guidelines For Delegates, which contains basic information and regulations for SCMUN VII. The New Guidelines For Delegates will inform the required preparation before the conference and the permissive behaviors during the conference for delegates. PDF

To be more specific, The New Guidelines For Delegates will guide you, including but not limited to:

i) What Are the Different Positions At SCMUN

ii) How To Research A Country, An Issue

iii) How To Write A Policy Statement

iv) How To Write A Draft Resolution

v) How To Write An Opening Speech

vi) How To Prepare Official Notepaper

vii) What Is Lobbying

viii) What Happen During Debate Time
